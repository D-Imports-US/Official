Shipping Policies


Shipping Times:

Shipping times vary depending on the customer's location. We estimate that orders will be delivered within 3 to 7 business days. Orders will be processed and shipped from Monday to Friday, except on holidays.

Shipping Costs:

Shipping costs are included in the total value of your purchased product or products. You can view shipping costs at checkout.

Shipping Method:

We use reliable courier services to deliver our customers' orders securely and efficiently. We will provide customers with a tracking number to trace their order online.

International Shipments:

We offer international shipping to all of United States And Mexico, but please note that shipping times and costs may vary.
               

Refund Policies


Returns:

We accept product returns within 30 days from the delivery date. Products must be unused and in their original condition with intact tags and packaging.

Return Process:

To request a return, the customer must contact our customer service and provide purchase details. We will review the request and, if approved, provide instructions on how to return the product.

Refunds:

We will process refunds within 10 business days after receiving and inspecting the returned product. The refund will be made through the same payment method used in the original purchase.

Shipping Costs:

Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is a result of our error.

Damaged or Defective Products:

If the product arrives damaged or defective, we will provide a prepaid shipping label for the return and offer a full refund or replacement of the product.
